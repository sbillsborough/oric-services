Damp Proofing

Oric Services understands damp proofing. We can advise, specify and guarantee all your damp proofing requirements. From a small area of capillary action rising damp in the corner of your room, to huge commercial premises we have a solution that meets your needs.

Condensation Control

In recent years the most common form of unwanted dampness in buildings is water from the air that forms as condensation. It is now becoming a major domestic problem, at Oric Services we can help you solve these problems quickly, cheaply and efficiently.
